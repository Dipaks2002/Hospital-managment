Department of General medicine



The Department of Internal Medicine is managed by an excellent and competent team of doctors who are dedicated in providing patient-centered, evidence-based medical care. The goal is to improve health of our society through basic biomedical research and clinical investigation, comprehensive and personalized patient care at affordable costs and the training of the next generation of specialist physicians.


The department currently consists of 34 staff members with 5 Professors, 3 Associate professors, 7 Assistant professors, 4 Senior residents and 15 Junior residents divided into 5 units.

Department of Cardiology



Our Department offers the latest innovations in cardiac care both to out-patients and in-patients of all ages. We provide state-of-the-art tests and procedures to help in giving accurate diagnosis and create treatment plan. We see patients with very common heart problems such as angina, valvular heart disease, congenital heart disease, peripheral arterial disease and heart failure. Our Cardiologists use a broad range of treatment options to give each patient the best care possible. These include life style modification recommendations, medications and interventional procedures.

Department of Anaesthesiology



Department of Anaesthesiology and Critical Care (established in 1993) provides anaesthetic services to all surgical specialities of Shrinidhi Hegde Charitable Hospital and also takes care of critically ill patients. These include General Surgery, Orthopaedics, ENT, Head & Neck, Ophthalmology, Obstetrics and Gynaecology and super specialities like Neurosurgery, Urology, Paediatric Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Craniofacial and Onco-surgeries. Department is in charge of 20 bedded multi-disciplinary Intensive Care Unit and a 16 bedded high dependence unit. The Department is actively involved in the acute and chronic pain relief services. The department runs a regular pain clinic, it has an outpatient pre anaesthesia clinic for evaluation and preparation of surgical patients for both outpatient and in-patient procedures, and a labour analgesia unit to take care of labour pain during childbirth. Outside the operation theatre, the department caters to the needs of other departments like radiological procedures and endoscopic procedures, ECT etc.


Department of Orthopaedics



Established in 1993. The department of Orthopaedics included in the M.B.B.S Course and offers Post graduate MS program in Orthopaedics. In addition the department of orthopaedics has recognized guides for the interdisciplinary Ph.D program of Shrinidhi Deemed to be University. The department has enough staff as per the requirements of the medical council of India (MCI). It tenders daily OPD services with specialty clinics and has high rate of occupancy in its in patients wards both male and female wards. It has adequate infrastructural facilities for providing clinical training for undergraduate and post graduate students. The departmental library has 148 titles and 26 periodicals.

Department of Physiotherapy



As professionals, physiotherapists are experts at providing physiotherapy treatment for: Preventing injury and disability; Managing acute and chronic conditions; Improving and maintaining optimal physical performance; Rehabilitating injury and the effects of disease or disability; Educating patients to prevent re-occurrence of an injury. Patients may be referred to or seek assistance from a physiotherapist for a variety of health issues and receive valuable assistance.
